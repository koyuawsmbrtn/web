My principles

1.

If you can't have it make it yourself

A lot of people think they can't have anything, because they don't even try. If you want something so bad just do it yourself. Windows sucks? Make your own OS. Your fridge broke? Repair it yourself. There is nothing you can't do.

2.

Keep it simple, one thing for every purpose

That's also a thing I hate about smartphones. If you bought a smartphone just because of the camera why didn't you buy a camera in the first place? I know it sounds stupid, but really having a thing that does a thing well instead of one thing that does everything bad equally, why not keeping it simple?

3.

Never look back

This is the hardest part. Especially quitting social media. It's like quitting smoking. If you had a good streak continue and improve. Never look back.
