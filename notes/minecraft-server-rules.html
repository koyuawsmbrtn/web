Minecraft server rules

Welcome to the leonie.lgbt Minecraft Server!

We’re glad to have you here! This server is a chill place for creative minds, builders, and adventurers alike. To keep everything fun and fair for everyone, we have a few rules in place. Please follow them to ensure an enjoyable experience for yourself and others. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse, and staff decisions are final.

General Rules

  1. Be respectful. No hate speech, harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior.
  2. No griefing or stealing. Respect other players’ builds and belongings.
  3. Keep chat friendly. No excessive swearing, spam, or NSFW content.
  4. No cheating, hacking, or exploiting. Play fair and don’t use mods that give unfair advantages.
  5. Follow staff instructions. If an admin or mod asks you to stop something, listen.
  6. No advertising. This includes other servers, social media spam, or self-promotion.
  7. No excessive PvP. Only engage in PvP if both players agree.
  8. Use common sense. If you think something might be against the rules, it probably is.

Building & World Rules

  1. Respect the environment. Don’t leave floating trees or mass destruction.
  2. No lag machines. Avoid large redstone contraptions or excessive entities.
  3. No offensive builds. This includes hate symbols, NSFW structures, or anything disruptive.

Additional Notes

  • Breaking the rules can result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
  • If you encounter issues, report them to staff.
  • Enjoy the game and help build a positive community!

By playing on the koyu.space Minecraft server, you agree to follow these rules. Have fun and happy crafting!
